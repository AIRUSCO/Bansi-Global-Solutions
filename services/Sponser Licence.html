We Help to Obtain a Sponsor License for Your Company

Eligibility Assessment

We conduct a thorough assessment to ensure your company meets the eligibility criteria for a sponsor license, including:
• Business Viability:Verifying your business operations and financial stability.
• HR Systems:Evaluating your human resources systems and policies to ensure compliance with UK Visas and Immigration (UKVI) requirements.

Application Preparation and Submission

We guide you through the sponsor license application process, including:
• Document Collection: Gathering necessary documents such as proof of business operations, bank statements, and relevant licenses
• Application Form: Completing and submitting the online sponsor license application form.

Compliance with UKVI Requirements

Ensuring your company adheres to UKVI standards and responsibilities, including:
• Record Keeping:Setting up systems to maintain accurate records of sponsored employees.
• Reporting Duties: Establishing procedures for reporting significant changes to UKVI, such as employee non-attendance or changes in employment status.

HR Policies and Procedures

We help you develop robust HR policies and procedures to meet sponsor license requirements, including:
• Recruitment Practices: Ensuring fair and compliant recruitment processes.
• Employee Monitoring: Implementing systems to monitor and report employee attendance and performance.

Training and Support

Providing training and support to ensure your team understands their responsibilities under the sponsor license, including:
• Workshops:Conducting workshops on sponsor license compliance and best practices.
• Ongoing Support: Offering ongoing advice and support to address any issues or queries related to your sponsor license.

Renewal and Maintenance

Helping you renew and maintain your sponsor license to continue hiring international talent:
• License Renewal:Assisting with the timely renewal of your sponsor license before it expires.
• Compliance Audits:Conducting periodic audits to ensure ongoing compliance with UKVI requirements.

By partnering with Bansi Global Solutions Ltd, you gain access to expert guidance and support for both setting up your private limited company in the UK and obtaining a sponsor license. Our comprehensive services are designed to streamline these processes, allowing you to focus on growing your business and achieving your strategic objectives.
