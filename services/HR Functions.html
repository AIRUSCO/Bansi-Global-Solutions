Human Resources Provision and Management of Human Resources Functions

At Bansi Global Solutions Ltd, our Human Resources (HR) provision and management services encompass a comprehensive suite designed to optimize your organization's most valuable asset: its people. Our approach to HR is systematic, strategic, and tailored to meet the specific needs of your business, ensuring you have the right people in the right roles and that they are engaged, productive, and aligned with your organizational goals.

Recruitment and Staffing

We provide end-to-end recruitment services to help you attract, select, and onboard top talent. This includes:
• Job Analysis and Design: We work with you to create detailed job descriptions and specifications, ensuring a clear understanding of the roles and responsibilities.
• Sourcing Candidates: Utilizing a mix of job boards, social media, recruitment agencies, and employee referrals, we find the best candidates for your open positions.
• Screening and Selection: Our rigorous screening process includes resume reviews, interviews, aptitude tests, and background checks to ensure you hire the most suitable candidates.
• Onboarding: We facilitate smooth onboarding processes that include orientation programs and initial training sessions to help new hires integrate quickly and effectively into your organization.

Training and Development

Our training programs are designed to enhance the skills, knowledge, and abilities of your employees, ensuring they can perform their roles effectively and are prepared for future challenges. Key services include:
